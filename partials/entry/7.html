Entry 7 - Participatory Design Experience

For my participatory design experience I would run a brainstorming session like a hackathon to promote better ways of teach langauages.

Prompt: With your team you will have one hour to design 4 lesson plans, on subjects of your choosing, comprised of mulitple activites, which could each be completed in roughly five to ten minutes.

The goal of this prompt would be to uncover new or less thought of ways of learning and beable to introduce unique and unconventional activities into our application.
