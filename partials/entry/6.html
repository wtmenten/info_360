Entry 6 - Language App, n + 1

My n+1 design aims to add an AI to our language app. This AI would be able to translate speech and text. Furthermore, it would also be able to proof-read text and give you feedback on your spelling, pronuciation and grammar.

In the main screen of the new feature users would be able to select the voice options for proofing or translating or fill out a text-box and select whether they want to proofed or translated.

This second screen shows the feedback the AI would give for a proof-read.

This third screen shows the feedback the AI would give for a translation.
